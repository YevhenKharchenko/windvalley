How to Play

It's not about rushing through - you've got to think ahead and plan your route carefully.

  • 01

    Observe

    Analyze the valley layout and shifting airflow patterns.

  • 02

    Activate

    Create safe routes by guiding the wind through floating structures.

  • 03

    Lead

    Help the flock stay together and avoid turbulent storm pockets.

  • 04

    Reach

    Bring your luminous companions to the sanctuary to complete the level.
