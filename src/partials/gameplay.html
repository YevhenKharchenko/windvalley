How to Play

  • Study the Terrain

    Look at the stone plateau and understand the layout.
    Find where the current begins and where it must reach.

  • Shape the Path

    Move and rotate stone segments to create a continuous channel.

  • Activate the Flow

    Flip the switch and watch the current travel through the carved paths.

  • Refine the Structure

    If the current breaks, adjust the stone layout and try again.

Advanced Challenges

  • Split currents that power multiple paths

  • Pressure thresholds that require stronger flow

  • Timed gates that open and close

  • Multi-layer platforms with vertical connections
