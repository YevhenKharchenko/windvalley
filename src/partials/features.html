Core Game Features

  • Wooden Plank Paths

    Tap to place planks of different shapes and orientations. Each plank determines how Stickmen will travel. Choosing the right shape at the right moment is key to success.

  • Adorable Stickmen

    Color-coded characters march cutely toward their destination. There's something really nice about seeing them all march along the route you built.

  • Challenging Puzzle Layouts

    Each level throws different layout puzzles at you. Dock space is limited, and poor planning can quickly cause chaos.

  • Special Mechanics

    Unlock routes with keys, pass through color gates, and avoid tricky roadblocks. Some levels introduce time pressure to increase tension.

  • Dynamic Harbor Maps

    Each stage features uniquely designed ships and docks. No two levels feel exactly the same.

  • Satisfying Board Clears

    When that last little guy finally makes it and your crazy plan actually worked? Pure dopamine hit right there.
